Primary Font Stack Samples

Since "Ripe Grotesque" is not available as a free web font, this demo showcases the best geometric grotesque alternatives from Google Fonts that provide similar characteristics and aesthetic qualities.

Primary Font (Cabin)

The Art of Slow Processes

Cabin provides excellent legibility and geometric qualities that work well for both headings and body text. The lowercase 'g' character closely matches the distinctive style found in premium grotesque fonts.

Display Font (Josefin Sans)

Contemplative Cyanotypes

Josefin Sans excels in display applications with its geometric elegance and rounded corners. Perfect for bold headlines and artistic statements.

Geometric Alternative (Montserrat)

Analog Photography Processes

Montserrat offers a clean, geometric approach with multiple weights available. Great for structured layouts and modern design elements.

Body Text Font (Lato)

Research and Artistic Practice

Lato provides excellent readability for longer text passages while maintaining a contemporary feel that complements the geometric headline fonts.

Implementation Notes

Why These Fonts Were Chosen

  • Cabin - Best match for geometric grotesque characteristics, particularly the distinctive lowercase 'g'
  • Josefin Sans - Excellent for bold display text with geometric elegance and rounded corners
  • Montserrat - Clean geometric alternative with comprehensive weight options
  • Lato - Highly legible for body text while maintaining contemporary feel

Usage Recommendations

  • Use --font-display for main headings and artistic statements
  • Use --font-primary for subheadings and secondary content
  • Use --font-body for longer text passages and descriptions
  • Use --font-geometric for structured elements and navigation
